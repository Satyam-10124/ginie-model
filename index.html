Dataset Goldmines

The open-source corpus that trains Ginie

Seven publicly available datasets cover Solidity, Daml, and security patterns. Combined they provide over 514k unique contracts — more than enough for a specialized model that beats general-purpose alternatives on smart contract generation tasks.

Three phases of data ingestion: immediate ready-to-train pairs on Day 2, scale phase on Days 7–8, and a security layer added on Days 13–14. Each phase feeds the same training pipeline.

Apollo + AI Automation Stack

Eight tools · $160/month · six webhook triggers · 24/7

Six Make.com webhook triggers connect Apollo signals to Claude actions, replacing 20+ hours of manual follow-up per week. The stack costs less than one investor dinner and runs while you sleep.

Six automation triggers run 24/7 via Make.com. Every hot signal gets an automated response within minutes — from Slack alert to Claude-drafted reply to auto-sent Calendly link.
Trigger: email opened 3× in 24 hours
Slack alert + priority bump
Contact moves to priority sequence. Slack fires instantly: "[Name] at [Firm] opened 4 times in 24 hours — act within 2 hours." Claude drafts a suggested follow-up. You review and send in one click.
Trigger: link clicked
Auto-send Calendly link
Claude generates a short warm follow-up with Calendly embedded. Sent within 15 minutes of the click. Converts investor curiosity to a booked meeting without manual intervention — even at 3am.
Trigger: reply received
Claude classifies + drafts reply
Claude reads the reply, classifies it (positive, objection, not interested, needs info), and drafts a tailored response. You review and send in one click. Response time under 30 minutes on any reply.
Trigger: meeting booked
Full AI meeting prep brief
Claude generates a 1-page brief: fund thesis, portfolio overlap with Canton, likely objections and your responses, questions to ask, what a yes looks like. Delivered to your inbox before every call.
Trigger: new contact added to Apollo
Overnight enrichment pipeline
Apollo API pulls contact data → Claude researches via web search → generates investor profile JSON with thesis, recent deals, Canton overlap, and personalisation hook → stored in Notion CRM for sequence use.
Trigger: 21 days no response
Archive + warm intro research
Contact archived. Claude researches second-degree connections — mutual contacts in the EVI user base, Canton ecosystem, or your LinkedIn network. Flags the best warm intro path for a follow-up attempt.
