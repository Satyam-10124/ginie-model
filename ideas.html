How liveness rewards work every 10 minutes
Round opens — every 10 minutes a new minting round starts
Coupons distributed — your validator receives a reward coupon based on liveness and activity during the round
Auto-minted next round — your node's automation redeems the coupon in the following round, minting CC into your wallet
Critical: If you miss a round (node offline), the coupon expires and is lost permanently. Uptime = money. Downtime = permanent loss.
The 170% return on fees — official Canton math
You pay $1.00 in traffic fees for a transaction
You get $0.20 back immediately as a rebate (20% of fees)
Your Featured App activity marker earns up to $1.50 in minting rights from the 62% pool
Total return: $0.20 + $1.50 = $1.70 earned on $1.00 spent = 170% return on fees paid
This requires: (a) you pay the traffic fee yourself rather than passing to user, and (b) the $1.50/txn cap is reached — which it is at current network size and CC price
